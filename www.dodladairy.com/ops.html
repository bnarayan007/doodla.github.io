Our Packing Stations

At present the company is having 11 milk processing and packaging plants spread across various places in Telangana, Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka states. The unit locations are Nellore, Palamaneru, Penumuru, Sattenapally, Badvel, Kurnool, Tanuku, Koppal and Tumkur. Also there is a Skimmed Milk Powder (SMP) manufacturing unit at Nellore, Andhra Pradesh State. The combined total capacity of all the 10 (Including SMP plant) processing and packaging plants is 12 lakh litres per day.
